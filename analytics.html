Key Insights & Use Cases

  • Price Prediction: XGBoost model for estimating car values based on features
  • Market Trends: Track how inventory and average prices change over time
  • Lifecycle Tracking: Monitor individual cars from listing to removal
  • Anomaly Detection: Identify suspiciously priced vehicles (potential deals or errors)
  • Damage Impact Analysis: Quantify how different damage types affect resale value
  • Inventory Forecasting: Predict future listings and market supply
  • Buyer Alerts: Set up notifications for specific makes/models at target prices
  • Depreciation Analysis: Study how car values change while listed
